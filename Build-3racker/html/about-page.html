Tracker or otherwise known as 3racker was founded by two innovators
Oluwatobi Amoniyan and Lebi Oluwasoji with the aim of providing solution
to employee time management and efficiency. With the constant distractions
that employees face on a daily via the web, our tool Tracker was introduced
to help monitor employees time and activities inn the office to help them stay
efficient.
Efficiency is the key to success. If there is a better way of approaching a
